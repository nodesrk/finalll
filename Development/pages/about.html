C17- Team 1

As a team of 5, we are the c17 class team responsible for creating the simple frontend of our camera shop. Our team members have a diverse set of skills and backgrounds, ranging from design to programming. Together, we are dedicated to creating a user-friendly and visually appealing website that showcases the best products from our camera shop. We are committed to working collaboratively and efficiently, ensuring that our project is delivered on time and meets the expectations of our customers. Our team is passionate about photography and the role it plays in capturing life's most memorable moments. We believe that every customer deserves the best experience when it comes to purchasing a camera, and we strive to make that experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible.
